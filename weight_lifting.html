Weightlifitng for the Elderly

Lifting weights may seem to some like it is only for athletes and bodybuilders but that is not true. Weightlifting is beneficial for anyone and can be done safely at all ages. Many folks who find themselves on the older side of life may think that they want to avoid weightlifting due to risk of injury but the benefits of weightlifting far outweigh the risk.

Benefits
  • Increase bone density
  • Lower blood pressure
  • Weight management
  • Increase balance
  • Lowers risk of injury
  • Improves heart health
  • Helps manage blood sugars
  • Promotes mobility and flexibility

Safety Guidelines

The value in weight lifting is found in being able to do the exercises safely. Some safety guidelines include breathing thorughout the exercise and ensuring that there a five points of contacts when lifting. There are more safety guidelines that can help ensure safety when lifting.
